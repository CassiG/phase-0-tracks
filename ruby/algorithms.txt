## Pseudocode instructions for Elevator Robot 

- When elevator doors open, move forward until elevator wall is reached 
- Turn 180 degrees so you are now facing the opposite elevator wall 
- Look to the left side of the elevator
- If elevator buttons are seen
    - Ask user what floor they would like
    - Locate button #1 in the elevator button section
    - Count up until user number is reached
    - Press the button with the same number as the user number
- Else if no elevator buttons are seen, look to the right side of the elevator
- If elevator buttons are seen
    - Ask user what floor they would like
    - Locate button #1 in the elevator button section
    - Count up until user number is reached
    - Press the button with the same number as the user number
- Wait for elevator to move and reach desired floor number
- Wait for doors to open
- Once doors open, move forward to exit elevator
- Stop Elavator Program

## Pseudocode for Feeding my Dog Breakfast Program

- Set Breakfast Time to 7:00am
- Check Time
- When 7:00am 
    - Move forward in kitchen until sink wall is hit
    - Reach above sink to cupboard
    - Open Cupboard
    - Pull out Dog Food Bin
    - Open Dog Food Bin 
    - Pull out one scoop of food
    - Turn right
    - Move along sink wall until you are in hallway
    - Turn left 
    - Look down to dog food bowl

    - If dog food bowl is present
        - Place scoop of food in dog bowl
        - Turn left 
        - Move forward back into kitchen
        - Move forward along sink wall back to food bin
        - Place food scoop in bin
        - Pick up dog food bin and place it in cupboard
        - Close cupboard
    - If no dog food bowl is present
        - Turn left 
        - Move forward back into kitchen
        - Move forward along sink wall until you hit dishwasher
        - Open dishwasher
        - Pull out dog food bowl
        - Put dog food bowl on floor
        - Place food scoop in bin
        - Turn right
        - Pick up dog food bin and place it in cupboard
        - Close cupboard
- Call to dog "Breakfast time!"
